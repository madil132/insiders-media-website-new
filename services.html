Capture System

Lead Capture & Identity Resolution

97% of your website visitors leave without filling out a form. This system identifies who they are by name, email, and phone and automatically reaches out before they forget you exist.

🔍

Anonymous Visitor Identification

Our identity resolution layer matches anonymous website visitors against proprietary data networks, revealing the names, emails, and phone numbers of people browsing your site without submitting a form.

📱

Automated SMS Outreach

The moment a visitor is identified, a personalized SMS sequence is triggered automatically reaching them on their phone within minutes of leaving your site, before a competitor does.

📧

Targeted Email Retargeting

Automated email sequences delivered to identified visitors with personalized content based on the specific pages they browsed dramatically increasing re-engagement rates over generic retargeting.

🎯

Behavioral Segmentation

Visitors are segmented by pages visited, time on site, and browsing patterns allowing outreach sequences to be tailored to their specific intent and position in the buying process.

🔄

CRM Integration & Lead Sync

Identified leads are automatically pushed into your CRM with full behavioral data attached giving your sales team context before they make first contact.

📊

Revenue Recovery Reporting

Full attribution dashboard showing how many visitors were identified, how many responded to outreach, and the revenue recovered from leads that would otherwise have been permanently lost.

How It Works

The Identity Resolution Process

  • Visitor lands on your website from any traffic source
  • Identity layer runs in background no form required
  • Visitor data matched against verified consumer database
  • SMS and/or email sequence triggered automatically within minutes
  • Personalized message references specific pages they visited
  • Lead captured into CRM with full behavioral context
